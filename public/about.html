About

Interactive web application that allows users to scout basketball games and view advanced statistics. Users are able to input stats from games, or use our interactive application to log stats real time during a game. All player and team statistics are stored in a database so any user can look them up our website.

Mission

Our Mission is to provide athletes, coaches and teams with the platform to gain the right partnership. Using our product athletes are able to show their stats and increase their visiblity to recruiters. Coaches are also able to conduct effective practices, understand their weakness and strengths, motivate their athletes, and instruct athletes in game strategies and techniques where they need it most.

Advanced Stats

True shooting percentage

A shooting percentage that factors in the value of three-point field goals and free throws in addition to conventional two-point field goals.

Effective field goal percentage

Measures field goal percentage adjusting for made 3-point field goals being 1.5 times more valuable than made 2-point field goals.

2 Point Field Goal Frequency

The percentage of opponent field goal attempts that fit the specified criteria.

3 Point Field Goal Frequency

The percentage of opponent field goal attempts of the specified criteria that are 3 point attempts.

Free Throw Attempt Rate

The number of free throw attempts a team shoots in comparison to the number of field goal attempts that team shoots.

Assist Ratio

Assist Ratio is the number of assists a player averages per 100 possessions used.

Turnover Ratio

The number of turnovers a player or team averages per 100 possessions used.

Offensive Rating

Measures a team's points scored per 100 possessions. On a player level this statistic is team points scored per 100 possessions while he is on court.

Turnover Ratio

A basketball statistic used to out a player's number of assists in context with the number of turnovers they have caused. It is intended to be representative of a player's ball control.

Game Score

Points in basketball that are used to keep track of the score in a game.
